# WaaP (Wallet as a Protocol)

WaaP is a blockchain wallet solution featuring one-click sign-on capabilities without traditional seed phrases. The platform uses Two-Party Computation architecture to distribute private keys across multiple parties, eliminating single points of compromise.

## Core Features

The wallet supports convenient onboarding through email, phone, social login, or biometric authentication. Its 2PC security model protects against malware and blind signing attacks by splitting cryptographic keys between two entities. WaaP operates across Ethereum, Sui, and EVM-compatible networks while offering transaction sponsorship through its Gas Tank feature.

## Key Differentiators

As a protocol-level solution rather than a hosted service, WaaP operates without recurring SaaS fees. The platform enables trustless account recovery — users recover access without relying on a custodian, providing security without custodial dependencies.
