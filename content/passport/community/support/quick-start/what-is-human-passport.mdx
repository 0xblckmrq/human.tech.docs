# What is Human Passport?

Human Passport is a modular suite of identity tools that enables Proof of Humanity in web3. The [Passport App](https://app.passport.xyz) allows users to collect Stamps — verifiable credentials representing activities that real humans can verify but bad actors struggle to fake.

## Stamp Categories

Stamps fall into four main categories:

- **Blockchain & Crypto** — On-chain activity and holdings
- **Government IDs** — Identity verification via KYC providers
- **Social & Professional Platforms** — Accounts on established platforms
- **Biometrics** — Biometric verification methods

## Unique Humanity Score

Each Stamp carries a specific weight. The sum of all your verified Stamps equals your **Unique Humanity Score**. A score of **20 or higher** typically indicates a unique human.

## Privacy-Preserving

Passport is designed to be privacy-preserving. No personally identifiable information (PII) is collected or stored. The system only verifies that certain credentials exist without exposing the underlying data.

## For Builders

Beyond the Passport App, Passport offers additional tools for builders:

- **Model-Based Detection** — Machine learning models for evaluating identity signals
- **Data Services** — APIs and tools for integrating Passport data into your applications
