# Custom Passport Dashboards and Scores

Partners can create **custom dashboards** tailored to their specific needs. These dashboards may feature:

- A reduced set of Stamp offerings relevant to the partner's use case.
- Unique score weights that differ from the default Passport scoring model.

## Cross-Dashboard Recognition

Stamps that you verify on the main Passport app at [app.passport.xyz](https://app.passport.xyz) remain recognized across all custom partner dashboards. You do not need to reverify Stamps for each partner -- your verified credentials carry over automatically.

Keep in mind that because custom dashboards may use different weights, your score on a partner dashboard may differ from your score on the main Passport app.
