# Using Human Passport with Partners

Partner applications integrate Human Passport to verify that their users are real people. Here is how the process works:

1. **Look for the connect button** -- Partner sites display a "Connect" or "Sign in with Ethereum" button.
2. **Connect your wallet** -- The app requests your Passport score from the Passport protocol.
3. **Score review** -- The partner reviews your score against their threshold. The recommended threshold is 20.
4. **Access granted** -- If your score meets or exceeds the threshold, you are granted access to the gated feature or community.

**Important:** You must use the same Ethereum address that you used when creating your Passport. If you connect with a different wallet, the partner application will not find your Passport data.
