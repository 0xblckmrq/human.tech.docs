# Creating a Passport

Follow these steps to create your Human Passport:

1. **Get an Ethereum wallet** -- If you do not already have one, set up an Ethereum-compatible wallet such as MetaMask.
2. **Visit the Passport app** -- Go to [app.passport.xyz](https://app.passport.xyz).
3. **Select "Sign in With Ethereum"** -- Click the sign-in button on the landing page.
4. **Connect your wallet and sign the message** -- Approve the connection in your wallet and sign the verification message when prompted.

Your Passport is associated with your Ethereum address. New users start with an empty Passport and can immediately begin collecting Stamps to build their humanity score.
