# Onchain Passport

You can optionally mint your Passport onchain for partner campaigns that require it.

## How It Works

Onchain Passport uses the **Ethereum Attestation Service** to create a verifiable attestation of your Passport data on the blockchain.

## Cost

Minting costs approximately **$3 plus gas fees**. Using a Layer 2 network such as **Optimism** is recommended to minimize gas costs.

## Benefits

- **Smart contract retrieval** -- Partner applications can read your Passport data directly from the blockchain via smart contracts.
- **Server independence** -- Your credentials exist on-chain and do not depend on any centralized server being available.
- **Permanent credentials** -- Your onchain attestation persists as a tamper-proof record on the blockchain.

## When to Mint

Not every user needs an onchain Passport. Mint yours when a partner application or campaign specifically requires onchain verification.
