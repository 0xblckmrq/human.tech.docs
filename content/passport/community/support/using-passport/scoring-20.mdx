# Scoring 20, for Humans

A score of 20 is the recommended threshold for most partner applications. Here is how to reach it.

## Highest-Weight Stamps

| Stamp | Max Points |
| --- | --- |
| ETH Activity | 22.541 |
| NFT Activity | 22.053 |
| Government ID | 16.026 |
| Identity Staking | 12.506 |
| Binance | 10.021 |

## Path for Crypto Beginners

If you are new to crypto and do not have extensive on-chain history, focus on these Stamps:

1. **Government ID** -- Up to 16.026 points.
2. **Identity Staking** -- Up to 12.506 points.
3. **GitHub** -- 6.026 points.
4. **Biometrics** -- Verify your biometric data for additional points.
5. **ZK Email** -- Verify your email via zero-knowledge proof.

## Free Stamps

Several Stamps can be claimed at no cost:

- **GitHub** -- 6.026 points
- **Google** -- 0.525 points
- Other social and web2 Stamps with lower weights

Combine multiple Stamps to cross the 20-point threshold. The more Stamps you verify, the higher and more resilient your score becomes.
