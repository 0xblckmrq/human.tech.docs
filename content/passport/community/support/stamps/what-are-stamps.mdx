# What are Stamps?

Stamps are verifiable credentials that prove ownership or activity on external platforms. They form the foundation of your Unique Humanity Score in Human Passport.

## Stamp Categories

Stamps are organized into four categories:

- **Blockchain & Crypto** — Wallet activity, token holdings, on-chain transactions
- **Government IDs** — Identity verification through KYC providers
- **Social & Professional** — Accounts on platforms like GitHub, LinkedIn, Discord, and more
- **Biometric Verification** — Physical identity verification methods

## How Stamps Work

When you verify a Stamp, the Passport App checks that the relevant data exists on the external platform. Your data is **never stored or exported** — the app simply confirms that the credential is valid.

## Stamp Expiration

Stamps expire every **90 days** to ensure that your credentials remain current and accurate. After expiration, you will need to re-verify your Stamps.

## One-Click Renewal

Returning users can take advantage of **one-click renewal** to quickly re-verify their previously connected Stamps without repeating the full verification process.
