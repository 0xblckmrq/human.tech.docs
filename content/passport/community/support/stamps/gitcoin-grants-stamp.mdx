# Gitcoin Grants Stamp

The Gitcoin Grants Stamp recognizes participation in Gitcoin Grants rounds as evidence of genuine community engagement.

## Eligibility Requirements

To qualify for the Gitcoin Grants Stamp, you must meet the following criteria:

- **Verified wallet** — Your wallet must be connected and verified in Passport
- **Minimum contribution** — At least **$1** contributed to a qualifying round
- **No suspicious flags** — Your account must not be flagged for suspicious activity
- **Score meets threshold** — Your contribution behavior must meet the scoring threshold

## Qualifying Rounds

Only **official Gitcoin-funded rounds** qualify for the Gitcoin Grants Stamp. Community-run or independent rounds on the Gitcoin platform may not be eligible.

## Verification Timeline

After a Gitcoin Grants round concludes, allow up to **4 weeks** for verification data to become available. The Stamp will not be verifiable until the round data has been fully processed.
