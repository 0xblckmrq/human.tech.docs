## How Weights Work

Your **Unique Humanity Score** is the sum of the weights from all your verified Stamps. A higher score provides stronger evidence that you are a unique human.

The recommended threshold of **20** means that partners integrating Passport should generally require users to have a combined Stamp weight of at least 20 to be considered verified.
