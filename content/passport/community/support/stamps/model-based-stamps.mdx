# Model-Based Stamps

Model-Based Stamps use machine learning to evaluate wallet transaction history and assign a score based on patterns associated with real human activity.

## ETH Stamp

The ETH Stamp assesses on-chain activity across multiple networks:

- Ethereum Mainnet
- Arbitrum
- Base
- Optimism
- Polygon
- zkSync

The model analyzes transaction patterns, frequency, and diversity to determine a score reflecting genuine human activity.

## NFT Stamp

The NFT Stamp focuses specifically on **Ethereum Mainnet** NFT activity, evaluating factors such as minting, trading, and holding patterns.

## Scoring Criteria

The specific scoring criteria for Model-Based Stamps are **kept confidential** to prevent gaming and manipulation. This ensures the integrity of the scoring system.

## Renewal

Like all Stamps, Model-Based Stamps renew every **90 days**. Your score may change upon renewal based on your latest on-chain activity.

## Important Note

The absence of points from a Model-Based Stamp does **not** imply that you are a Sybil or bad actor. It simply means there is insufficient on-chain data to assign a score. You can still build your Unique Humanity Score through other Stamp categories.
