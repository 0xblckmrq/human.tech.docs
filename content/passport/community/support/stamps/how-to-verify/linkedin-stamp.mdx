# LinkedIn Stamp

The LinkedIn Stamp verifies that you own a LinkedIn account through a standard OAuth flow.

## Quick Facts

| Detail | Value |
| --- | --- |
| **Cost** | Free |
| **Time** | ~1 minute |
| **Deduplication** | LIFO (Last In, First Out) |

## Requirements

- A LinkedIn account

## Step-by-Step Verification

1. Navigate to the Passport App and connect your wallet.
2. Select the LinkedIn Stamp.
3. Click the verification button to initiate the LinkedIn OAuth flow.
4. Log in to your LinkedIn account and authorize the Passport App.
5. The stamp will be added to your Passport.

## Deduplication

The LinkedIn Stamp uses **LIFO (Last In, First Out)** deduplication:

- Only one Passport can hold the LinkedIn Stamp per LinkedIn account.
- If you verify the stamp on a new Passport, it will be automatically removed from any previously linked Passport.

## Troubleshooting

- **OAuth failed** — Try clearing your browser cookies or using an incognito window.
- **Wrong account** — If you have multiple LinkedIn accounts, ensure you are signing in with the intended one during the OAuth flow.
- **Stamp removed from old Passport** — This is expected behavior due to LIFO deduplication. Each LinkedIn account can only be linked to one Passport at a time.
