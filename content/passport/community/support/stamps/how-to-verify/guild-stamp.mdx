# Guild.xyz Stamp

The Guild.xyz Stamp verifies that you are an owner or admin of a guild on Guild.xyz with a substantial membership base.

## Quick Facts

| Detail | Value |
| --- | --- |
| **Cost** | Free |
| **Time** | ~10 minutes |
| **Validity** | 90 days |
| **Credential** | Guild Admin |

## Requirements

- You must be an **owner or admin** of a guild on Guild.xyz
- The guild must have **250 or more members**

## Step-by-Step Verification

1. Navigate to the Passport App and connect your wallet.
2. Select the Guild.xyz Stamp.
3. Initiate the verification process.
4. The system will check your wallet against Guild.xyz to confirm you are an owner or admin of a qualifying guild.
5. If your guild has 250+ members and you hold the appropriate role, the stamp will be added to your Passport.

## Important Notes

- **Admin or owner role required** — Simply being a member of a large guild does not qualify. You must have admin or owner privileges.
- **250-member threshold** — The guild must have at least 250 members at the time of verification.
- **90-day validity** — The stamp expires after 90 days and must be re-verified.

## Troubleshooting

- **Guild not detected** — Ensure your wallet is the one associated with your Guild.xyz admin/owner role.
- **Not enough members** — The guild must have 250 or more members. Check the member count on Guild.xyz.
- **Role not recognized** — Confirm your role is set to owner or admin within the guild settings.
