# Discord Stamp

The Discord Stamp verifies that you own an established Discord account, indicating genuine community participation.

## Quick Facts

| Detail | Value |
| --- | --- |
| **Cost** | Free |
| **Time** | ~2 minutes |
| **Validity** | 90 days |
| **Deduplication** | LIFO (Last In, First Out) |

## Requirements

Your Discord account must meet **all** of the following criteria:

- **Account age** — 1 year or older
- **Server membership** — Member of 10 or more servers
- **Verified connections** — 2 or more verified connections on your Discord profile (e.g., GitHub, Twitter, Steam)

## Step-by-Step Verification

1. Navigate to the Passport App and connect your wallet.
2. Select the Discord Stamp.
3. Click the verification button to initiate the OAuth flow.
4. Log in to your Discord account and authorize the Passport App.
5. The system will check your account against the requirements.
6. If all criteria are met, the stamp will be added to your Passport.

## Deduplication

The Discord Stamp uses **LIFO (Last In, First Out)** deduplication:

- Only one Passport can hold the Discord Stamp per Discord account.
- Verifying on a new Passport automatically removes the stamp from any previously linked Passport.

## Troubleshooting

- **Account too new** — Your Discord account must be at least 1 year old. There is no way to bypass this requirement.
- **Not enough servers** — Join more servers to meet the 10-server minimum.
- **Missing verified connections** — Add verified connections in Discord Settings > Connections. You need at least 2.
- **OAuth error** — Try clearing your browser cache or using a different browser.
