# Proof of Clean Hands Stamp

The Proof of Clean Hands Stamp proves that you are not on any international sanction lists and are not a politically exposed person (PEP), using zero-knowledge proofs to preserve your privacy.

## Quick Facts

| Detail | Value |
| --- | --- |
| **Cost** | ~$5 |
| **Time** | ~10 minutes |
| **Privacy** | Zero-knowledge proofs |
| **Stamp Validity** | 90 days |
| **Credential Validity** | 1 year |
| **Supported Networks** | Ethereum, Optimism, Avalanche, Base |

## Requirements

- ~$5 for the verification fee
- A wallet connected to your Passport

## What It Proves

This stamp cryptographically verifies that you:

- Are **not on any international sanction lists**
- Are **not a politically exposed person** (PEP)

All verification is done using **zero-knowledge proofs**, meaning the system confirms compliance without accessing or storing your personal data.

## Step-by-Step Verification

1. Navigate to the Passport App and connect your wallet.
2. Select the Proof of Clean Hands Stamp.
3. Follow the verification flow to generate a zero-knowledge proof of your compliance status.
4. Pay the ~$5 verification fee.
5. Once verified, the credential is issued and the stamp is added to your Passport.

## Validity and Renewal

- The **stamp** on your Passport is valid for **90 days** before it must be re-verified.
- The underlying **credential** is valid for **1 year**.
- To renew, repeat the verification process and pay the fee again.

## Supported Networks

The credential is recognized across:

- Ethereum
- Optimism
- Avalanche
- Base

## Troubleshooting

- **Verification failed** — If the verification process fails, ensure your wallet is properly connected and try again.
- **Stamp expired** — Re-verify through the same process every 90 days to maintain an active stamp.
- **Fee payment issue** — Ensure you have sufficient funds on the network used for the verification fee.
