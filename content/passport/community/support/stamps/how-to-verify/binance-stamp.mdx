# Binance Stamp

The Binance Stamp verifies that you hold a Binance Account Bound Token (BABT), proving you have a verified Binance account.

## Quick Facts

| Detail | Value |
| --- | --- |
| **Cost** | 1 USDT |
| **Time** | ~5 minutes |
| **Network** | BNB Smart Chain (BSC) |
| **Validity** | Until revoked |
| **Deduplication** | LIFO (Last In, First Out) |

## Requirements

- A verified Binance account (KYC completed)
- A Binance Account Bound Token (BABT) minted to your wallet
- BNB for gas fees on BSC
- 1 USDT for the verification fee

## Step-by-Step Verification

1. Complete KYC verification on your Binance account if you have not already.
2. Mint the BABT token through Binance to your wallet address.
3. Navigate to the Passport App and connect the wallet holding the BABT.
4. Select the Binance Stamp and begin the verification process.
5. Sign the transaction to confirm ownership of the wallet.
6. The stamp will be added to your Passport once verification is complete.

## Deduplication

The Binance Stamp uses **LIFO (Last In, First Out)** deduplication. This means:

- Only one Passport can hold the Binance Stamp per Binance account at any time.
- If you verify the stamp on a new Passport, it will be removed from any previously linked Passport.

## Revocation

If you need to revoke your Binance Stamp, there is a mandatory **72-hour waiting period** before the revocation takes effect. This cooldown period is a security measure to prevent abuse.

## Troubleshooting

- **BABT not detected** — Ensure the BABT is minted to the same wallet address connected to your Passport.
- **Transaction failed** — Confirm you have enough BNB for gas fees and 1 USDT for the verification fee on BSC.
- **KYC not complete** — You must fully complete Binance identity verification before minting the BABT.
