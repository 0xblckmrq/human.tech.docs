# Phone Verification Stamp

The Phone Verification Stamp verifies ownership of a real phone number through Human ID, with a Soulbound Token (SBT) minted on Optimism.

## Quick Facts

| Detail | Value |
| --- | --- |
| **Cost** | $5 + gas fees |
| **Time** | ~5 minutes |
| **Provider** | Human ID |
| **SBT Network** | Optimism |
| **Supported Networks** | Ethereum, Optimism, Base, Aurora, Avalanche, Fantom |

## Requirements

- A real phone number (burner/virtual numbers are not accepted)
- $5 for the verification fee
- Gas fees for SBT minting on Optimism

## Step-by-Step Verification

1. Navigate to the Passport App and connect your wallet.
2. Select the Phone Verification Stamp.
3. You will be redirected to Human ID for phone verification.
4. Enter your phone number.
5. Receive and enter the OTP (one-time password) sent via **SMS**, **WhatsApp**, or **Viber**.
6. Once verified, an SBT will be minted on Optimism.
7. The stamp will be added to your Passport.

## OTP Delivery Methods

You can receive the one-time password through any of the following channels:

- **SMS** — Standard text message
- **WhatsApp** — Via WhatsApp messaging
- **Viber** — Via Viber messaging

## Important Notes

- **Real phone numbers only** — Virtual phone numbers, burner phones, and VoIP numbers are not accepted. The system detects and rejects these.
- **One phone per Passport** — Each phone number can only be linked to one Passport.
- **SBT is non-transferable** — The Soulbound Token minted during verification cannot be transferred to another wallet.

## Supported Networks

While the SBT is minted on Optimism, the stamp is recognized across:

- Ethereum
- Optimism
- Base
- Aurora
- Avalanche
- Fantom

## Troubleshooting

- **OTP not received** — Try a different delivery method (SMS, WhatsApp, or Viber). Check that your phone number is correct and can receive messages.
- **Number rejected** — Virtual and burner numbers are blocked. Use a real mobile phone number.
- **SBT not appearing** — The minting transaction may take a few minutes on Optimism. Check the transaction status on the Optimism block explorer.
