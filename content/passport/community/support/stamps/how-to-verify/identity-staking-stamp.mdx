# Identity Staking (GTC) Stamp

The Identity Staking Stamp allows you to stake GTC tokens on your identity or have others stake on your behalf, demonstrating skin-in-the-game for your Proof of Humanity.

## Quick Facts

| Detail | Value |
| --- | --- |
| **Cost** | 5 - 125 GTC (depending on tier) |
| **Networks** | Ethereum, Optimism, Arbitrum |

## Self-Staking Tiers

Stake GTC on your own identity to earn a self-staking credential:

| Tier | GTC Required |
| --- | --- |
| **Bronze** | 5 GTC |
| **Silver** | 20 GTC |
| **Gold** | 125 GTC |

## Community Staking Tiers

Other community members can stake GTC on your identity, earning you a community staking credential:

| Tier | Description |
| --- | --- |
| **Beginner** | Entry-level community trust |
| **Experienced** | Moderate community trust |
| **Trusted Citizen** | High community trust |

## Step-by-Step Verification

### Self-Staking

1. Acquire the required amount of GTC tokens on Ethereum, Optimism, or Arbitrum.
2. Navigate to the Passport App and connect your wallet.
3. Select the Identity Staking Stamp.
4. Choose the self-staking option and select your desired tier.
5. Approve the GTC token spend and confirm the staking transaction.
6. The stamp will be added to your Passport at the corresponding tier.

### Community Staking

1. Share your Passport profile with community members who want to stake on your identity.
2. Community members stake GTC on your identity through the Passport App.
3. As the total community stake on your identity reaches higher thresholds, your community staking tier increases.

## Supported Networks

You can stake GTC on any of the following networks:

- Ethereum
- Optimism
- Arbitrum

## Troubleshooting

- **Insufficient GTC** — Ensure you have enough GTC in your wallet on one of the supported networks.
- **Transaction failed** — Confirm you have enough native tokens (ETH) for gas fees on the chosen network.
- **Tier not updating** — Community staking tiers update as cumulative stakes reach new thresholds. Check the current total staked on your identity.
