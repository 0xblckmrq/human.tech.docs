# Steam Stamp

The Steam Stamp verifies your gaming activity on Steam, ensuring a diverse and genuine play history.

## Quick Facts

| Detail | Value |
| --- | --- |
| **Cost** | Free |
| **Time** | ~3 minutes |
| **Profile Requirement** | Public during verification |

## Requirements

Your Steam account must meet **all** of the following criteria:

- **100+ hours** of total playtime
- **10+ achievements** earned
- **3+ games** with at least 1 hour of playtime each
- **No single game** accounts for more than 80% of your total playtime
- **Public profile** — Your Steam profile and game details must be set to public during verification

## Step-by-Step Verification

1. Set your Steam profile to **public** (Profile > Edit Profile > Privacy Settings).
2. Ensure your **game details** are also set to public.
3. Navigate to the Passport App and connect your wallet.
4. Select the Steam Stamp.
5. Log in to your Steam account through the verification flow.
6. The system will check your playtime, achievements, and game diversity.
7. The stamp will be added to your Passport if all criteria are met.

## Diversity Requirement

The 80% rule ensures that your gaming activity is diverse. If a single game accounts for more than 80% of your total playtime, the stamp will not be granted. This prevents abuse from users who idle in a single game to accumulate hours.

## Important Notes

- **Set profile to public before verifying** — The system cannot read your gaming data if your profile is private or friends-only.
- **Game details must be public** — In addition to your overall profile, the game details section must also be set to public.
- You may set your profile back to private after verification is complete.

## Troubleshooting

- **Requirements not met** — Check your Steam profile to verify your hours, achievements, and game count.
- **Profile appears private** — Double-check both the overall profile privacy and the game details privacy in Steam settings.
- **Single game dominance** — If one game has more than 80% of your playtime, play other games to diversify your activity before re-verifying.
