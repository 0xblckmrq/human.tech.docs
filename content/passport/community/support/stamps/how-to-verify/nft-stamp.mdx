# NFT Stamp

The NFT Stamp recognizes your NFT holdings on Ethereum Mainnet, with tiered credentials based on the depth of your collection.

## Quick Facts

| Detail | Value |
| --- | --- |
| **Cost** | Free (plus any fees to mint/acquire NFTs) |
| **Time** | ~3 minutes |
| **Network** | Ethereum Mainnet |
| **Supported Standards** | ERC-721, ERC-1155 |

## Tiers

The NFT Stamp offers tiered credentials based on your NFT holdings:

| Tier | Description |
| --- | --- |
| **NFT Holder** | Owns at least one qualifying NFT |
| **Digital Collector** | Holds a modest NFT collection |
| **Art Aficionado** | Holds a substantial NFT collection |
| **NFT Visionary** | Holds an extensive NFT collection |

## Requirements

- One or more NFTs (ERC-721 or ERC-1155) held in your wallet on Ethereum Mainnet
- The wallet holding the NFTs must be connected to your Passport

## Step-by-Step Verification

1. Navigate to the Passport App and connect the wallet holding your NFTs.
2. Select the NFT Stamp.
3. The system will scan your wallet for ERC-721 and ERC-1155 tokens on Ethereum Mainnet.
4. Based on your holdings, you will be assigned the appropriate tier.
5. The stamp will be added to your Passport.

## Important Notes

- **Ethereum Mainnet only** — NFTs on other networks (Polygon, Arbitrum, etc.) are not currently counted.
- **ERC-721 and ERC-1155** — Only these two token standards are recognized.
- **Wallet must hold NFTs** — The NFTs must be in the wallet at the time of verification. NFTs in escrow, staked, or listed for sale on a marketplace may not be detected.

## Troubleshooting

- **NFTs not detected** — Ensure your NFTs are ERC-721 or ERC-1155 tokens on Ethereum Mainnet and are held in the connected wallet.
- **Lower tier than expected** — The tier is based on qualifying NFT holdings. Not all tokens may meet the criteria.
- **Wrong network** — Only Ethereum Mainnet NFTs are counted for this stamp.
