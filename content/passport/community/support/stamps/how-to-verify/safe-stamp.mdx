# Safe Stamp

The Safe Stamp verifies that you are an owner or signer of a Safe (formerly Gnosis Safe) multisig account on Ethereum Mainnet.

## Quick Facts

| Detail | Value |
| --- | --- |
| **Cost** | Free (excluding gas fees) |
| **Time** | ~3 minutes |
| **Network** | Ethereum Mainnet |

## Requirements

- You must be an **owner or signer** of a Safe account on Ethereum Mainnet
- The wallet connected to your Passport must be listed as an owner/signer on the Safe

## Step-by-Step Verification

1. Navigate to the Passport App and connect the wallet that is an owner/signer of a Safe account.
2. Select the Safe Stamp.
3. The system will check if your connected wallet is an owner or signer on any Safe account on Ethereum Mainnet.
4. Sign a message to confirm wallet ownership.
5. The stamp will be added to your Passport.

## Important Notes

- **Ethereum Mainnet only** — Safe accounts on other networks (Polygon, Arbitrum, etc.) are not currently supported.
- **Owner/signer role required** — Simply interacting with a Safe is not enough. Your wallet must be listed as an owner or signer.
- **Gas fees** — While the stamp itself is free, any on-chain interactions required during verification will incur standard Ethereum gas fees.

## Troubleshooting

- **Safe not detected** — Confirm your wallet address is listed as an owner or signer on the Safe at [app.safe.global](https://app.safe.global).
- **Wrong network** — Only Safe accounts on Ethereum Mainnet are supported.
- **Not an owner** — If you were removed as a signer from the Safe, the stamp will no longer be verifiable with your wallet.
