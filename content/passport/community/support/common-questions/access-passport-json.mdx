# How Can I Access My Passport JSON?

Your Passport JSON enables data exchange and portability. Follow these steps to access it:

1. **Connect your wallet** -- Sign in to the Passport app at [app.passport.xyz](https://app.passport.xyz).
2. **Navigate to your dashboard** -- Go to your main Passport dashboard.
3. **Select Passport Details** -- Click on the Passport Details option.
4. **View the JSON popup** -- A popup will display your Passport data in JSON format.
5. **Click Download** -- Save the JSON file to your device.
