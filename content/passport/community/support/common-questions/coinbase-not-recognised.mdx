# Coinbase Account Not Recognised

If your Coinbase account is not being recognised when attempting to claim the Coinbase Stamp, the issue is likely a wallet address mismatch.

## What to Check

Visit the Coinbase verification page and confirm that the **Associated Address** matches the wallet address you used to create your Passport. If the addresses do not match, the Passport app will not be able to verify your Coinbase credential.

Update your Coinbase attestation to point to the correct wallet address, then retry the Stamp verification in the Passport app.
