# Binance BABT - Why Can't I Claim the Stamp?

If you are unable to claim the Binance BABT Stamp, the most common cause is a **wallet mismatch** between the wallet holding your BABT and the wallet you used to create your Passport.

## How to Fix It

1. **Revoke your existing BABT** -- Remove the BABT token from your current wallet through the Binance interface.
2. **Wait 72 hours** -- Binance requires a 72-hour waiting period before you can re-mint.
3. **Re-mint on the correct wallet** -- Mint your BABT to the same wallet address associated with your Passport.

After re-minting, you should be able to claim the Binance BABT Stamp in the Passport app.
