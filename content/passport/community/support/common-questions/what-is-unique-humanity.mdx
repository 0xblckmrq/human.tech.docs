# What is Unique Humanity?

**Unique Humanity** is a verification system that ensures digital identities represent actual, distinct individuals. It works by aggregating evidence from multiple sources to produce a confidence score.

## How It Works

- **Stamps from web2 and web3 sources** -- Users verify credentials from providers like GitHub, Google, government ID services, on-chain activity, and more.
- **Score calculation** -- Your score is the sum of the weights assigned to each verified Stamp.
- **Threshold-based access** -- Applications set a minimum score to gate access, ensuring participants are real people.

## Applications

Unique Humanity verification is used across many contexts:

- **Funding distribution** -- Ensuring that grants and quadratic funding go to real contributors.
- **Governance** -- Preventing Sybil attacks on DAO votes and community decisions.
- **Community protection** -- Keeping communities free of bots and fake accounts.
