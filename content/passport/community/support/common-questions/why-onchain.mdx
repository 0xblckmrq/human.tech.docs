# Why Should We Put Our Passport Onchain?

Putting your Passport onchain creates a **tamper-proof blockchain record** of your verified credentials. This record is transparent, permanent, and independently verifiable.

## When It Matters

Some partner applications access Passport data directly from the blockchain rather than through the Passport API. In these cases, having an onchain Passport is required for the application to read and verify your score.

If a partner campaign or application specifically requires onchain verification, minting your Passport ensures compatibility.
