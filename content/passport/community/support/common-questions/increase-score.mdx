# How Can I Increase My Passport Score?

The most direct way to increase your Passport score is to **verify more Stamps**. Each Stamp has a specific weight, and your score improves proportionally as you add verified credentials.

Visit the Passport app at [app.passport.xyz](https://app.passport.xyz) to see which Stamps are available and how many points each one contributes.

For a full breakdown of Stamp weights, see the [Stamp weights page](/passport/community/support/stamps/stamp-weights).
