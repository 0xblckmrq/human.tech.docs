# What is a Stamp vs Credential?

A **Stamp** is one of approximately 20 partner verification providers available in Passport. Each Stamp can contain **one or multiple credentials**, where each credential represents a specific level of verification within that provider.

## Example: ETH Activity Stamp

The ETH Activity Stamp contains three credentials:

| Credential | Points |
| --- | --- |
| ETH Enthusiast | 10 |
| ETH Advocate | 2 |
| ETH Maxi | 2 |

When you verify the ETH Activity Stamp, you may qualify for one, two, or all three credentials depending on your on-chain history. Your score reflects only the credentials you have earned within each Stamp.
