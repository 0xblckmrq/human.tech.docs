# Why Have My Stamps Expired?

Stamps expire every **90 days**. This expiration policy serves two purposes:

1. **Maintaining confidence** -- Regular reverification ensures that Stamps still reflect your current credentials and identity.
2. **Increasing Sybil attack difficulty** -- Expiration forces attackers to continuously re-verify, making sustained Sybil attacks more costly.

Additionally, expiration allows users to **reassociate Stamps with a different wallet** if needed. After a Stamp expires on one wallet, it can be claimed on another.

When your Stamps expire, simply revisit the Passport app and reverify them to restore your score.
