# How is My Score Calculated?

Your Passport score is the **sum of all verified Stamp and credential points**. Each Stamp provider has an assigned weight, and when you successfully verify a Stamp, its points are added to your total score.

For a detailed breakdown of each Stamp's weight, refer to the [Passport Stamp weights page](/passport/community/support/stamps/stamp-weights).
