# My Stamp is Verified. Why Aren't the Scores Being Added?

If your Stamp shows as verified but is not contributing points to your score, the most likely cause is that the **account or token is already linked to another Passport**.

## How Deduplication Works

Passport uses a **LIFO (Last In, First Out) deduplication** model. Each account or token can only be associated with one Passport at a time. If you previously linked the same account to a different wallet's Passport, the newer claim will show as verified but will not add points.

## What to Do

You must wait **90 days** for the original Stamp to expire on the other Passport. Once expired, the credential becomes available and your verified Stamp will begin contributing points.
