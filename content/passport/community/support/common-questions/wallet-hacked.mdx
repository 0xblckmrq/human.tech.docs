# Wallet Hacked - Can I Move Scores to New Passport?

If your wallet has been compromised, most Stamps are transferable to a new Passport. Here is what to do:

## General Recovery Steps

1. **Create a new Passport** -- Set up a new Passport with a secure wallet.
2. **Reverify your Stamps** -- Go through the Stamp verification process again using the same accounts (GitHub, Google, etc.) on your new Passport.
3. **Submit to partners** -- Partners will see your accurate scores when you connect your new Passport.
4. **Wait 90 days for app display correction** -- It may take up to 90 days for the deduplication system to fully release Stamps from your old wallet.

## Special Cases

- **Binance BABT** -- Revoke the BABT on the compromised wallet, wait 72 hours, then re-mint on your new wallet.
- **Coinbase** -- Create a new attestation pointing to your new wallet address.
- **human.tech SBTs** -- Soulbound tokens from human.tech require a 1-year waiting period before they can be reassociated.
