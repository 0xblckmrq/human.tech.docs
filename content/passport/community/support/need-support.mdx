# Need Support?

If you need help with Passport, there are a couple of ways to get assistance.

## Check the FAQ

Many common questions are already answered in our FAQ sections. Browse the relevant topic areas to find answers to frequently asked questions about the Passport App, Stamps, scoring, staking, and more.

## Support Chat

If you cannot find the answer you need in the FAQ, you can use the **support chat icon** located in the **bottom right corner** of the page and the Passport App. Click the chat icon to start a conversation with our support team, and they will help you resolve your issue.
